Where The Signal Comes From

No Single Source Tells the Whole Story. We Watch All of Them.

Wastewater project intelligence is scattered across SRF intended-use plans, federal loan programs, consent decrees, state agency newsrooms, and local reporting. ClearFlow consolidates over 20 different channels into one feed.

EPA Clean Water State Revolving Fund (CWSRF)

State Intended Use Plans and project priority lists disclose funded wastewater projects months before they reach construction.

epa.gov/cwsrf

EPA WIFIA Program

Water Infrastructure Finance and Innovation Act loan announcements name specific large-scale wastewater projects and their financing structure.

epa.gov/wifia

USDA Rural Development Water & Environmental Programs

The primary federal loan/grant channel for wastewater projects in communities of 10,000 people or fewer.

rd.usda.gov/water-environmental-programs

State SRF Intended Use Plans & Priority Lists

Every state maintains its own ranked list of CWSRF-eligible wastewater projects, disclosed well ahead of any bid.

waterboards.ca.gov/srf

EPA Enforcement & Compliance History Online (ECHO)

Consent decrees and administrative orders tied to Clean Water Act violations frequently mandate capital upgrades on a fixed schedule.

echo.epa.gov

NACWA — National Association of Clean Water Agencies

Policy and funding updates from the trade association representing public clean water utilities nationwide.

nacwa.org

Water Environment Federation (WEF) & Member Associations

WEFTEC and regional WEA technical programs frequently preview major capital projects before they're publicly bid.

wef.org

SAM.gov Federal Contract Opportunities

Catches federally funded wastewater work, including USDA- and EPA-funded projects on tribal and federal lands.

sam.gov/opportunities

ENR & Dodge Construction Network

Engineering News-Record's design-firm rankings and Dodge's pre-planning project data track who's designing what, and where.

enr.com·construction.com

Trade & Engineering Publications

Water Finance & Management and WaterWorld cover funding awards, design milestones, and construction starts as they happen.

waterfm.com·waterworld.com

Engineering & EPC Firm Newsrooms

Direct monitoring of project-win announcements from the specialist design and construction firms themselves.

Black & Veatch·Jacobs·CDM Smith

Municipal Bond Offering Statements (EMMA/MSRB)

Utility revenue-bond offering statements disclose capital project scope and cost ahead of construction.

emma.msrb.org

County & Municipal Capital Improvement Plans

Multi-year CIPs disclose planned plant upgrades and expansions well before bidding.

gfoa.org/capital-improvement-planning

USACE Environmental Infrastructure Program

Congressionally designated assistance funds wastewater infrastructure in select communities, tracked separately from EPA/USDA channels.

usace.army.mil/Civil-Works

FEMA Hazard Mitigation / BRIC Program

Flood-resilience upgrades to treatment plants in floodplains are increasingly funded through FEMA hazard mitigation grants.

fema.gov/grants/mitigation/bric

ASCE Infrastructure Report Card

State-by-state wastewater infrastructure grades highlight underfunded systems likely to need near-term capital investment.

infrastructurereportcard.org/wastewater

State Environmental & Infrastructure Finance Agency Newsrooms

PENNVEST, Michigan EGLE, Wisconsin DNR, South Dakota DANR, and dozens of other state agencies publish funding-round press releases naming individual projects and dollar amounts.

PENNVEST·Michigan EGLE·South Dakota DANR

Governor's Office Press Releases

State funding-round announcements are frequently issued directly from the governor's office alongside the implementing agency.

governor.wv.gov·evers.wi.gov

Indian Health Service Sanitation Facilities Construction Program

The primary federal channel for tribal wastewater infrastructure funding, tracked separately from EPA and USDA programs.

ihs.gov/dsfc

State & Local Bid/Procurement Networks

Public notices and RFPs on state and municipal bid networks surface projects at the design-build and construction-bidding stage before award.

bidnetdirect.com

Regional & Local News Coverage

Local television and newspaper reporting frequently breaks groundbreakings, bid results, and funding awards well before trade press picks them up.

ENR Regional Editions·Governing
